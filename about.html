Welcome to Kenshin Impact
Kenshin Impact is an open-world, action RPG developed and published by miHoYoMan for PC, iOS/Android, and PS4/PS5 platforms. The game is Free-to-Play with a Gacha monetization system in the form of Wishes.
What to expect from this game
Open World
Explore the world of Tevyat! From the green pastures of Mondstat, to the serene harbour of LiYue. You will encounter many types of people along your journey, some of will be interested with your travels. Just be sure not to let your guard down! There are some who would attack you on sight!
Intense Combat
Master the sword and strike through your enemies, or use magic to blast them into dust! Use the 7 elements present in Teyvat and strategize your attacks by utilizing your opponents weaknesses! Dodge incoming attacks, and counter them with your skills! Destroy the enemies of Teyvat!
Variety of Casts
Along your journey, you will meet with many people along the way. Some of them might be interested to join you on your quest, and will support you with their powers. Build your team from an ensemble of unique and interesting characters. Improve them and they will help you with your goals!
Immerse Yourself
Explore Teyvat with all that it has to offer. Complete quests and make your way through Mondstat and LiYue. Gather strong allies and beat your enemies with your might! The world of Kenshin Impact is waiting for you to play now!
